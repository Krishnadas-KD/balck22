ABOUT BLACK 22

Black 22 COFFEE BAR AND ROASTERY, started as a homegrown brand and has blossomed into an iconic destination for coffee lovers, featuring two community-driven spaces in Riffa and Manama.

The specialty coffee bar is dedicated to crafting each cup to perfection using high quality beans (100% Arabica) devloped by our Coffee Roasters simply because we deeply care about the quality of the coffee we serve - from crop to cup - with the core aim of always satisfying our coffee lovers.

On the otherside the café as well provides a cozy ambiance and a welcoming environment for everyone,Whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply enjoying some solitude.
